Manicure Techniques

Gel

It utilizes a UV light to solidify and strengthen liquid polish, thereby extending the longevity of a gel manicure compared to traditional polish by up to three times.

The only drawback is that you'll need to return to the salon for its removal, as attempting to remove it yourself could harm your nails.

Acrylic

An acrylic manicure offers the flexibility to tailor its appearance to your preferences of styles and shapes.

This process typically involves the application of a blend of powder and liquid formulas over both your natural nails or false tips.

Shellac

Like gel, it needs to be cured under a UV or LED light. Shellac is particularly beneficial for individuals with weaker nails, offering the durability of gel manicures with easier removal.

This is what makes it a great choice for those seeking both strength and nail health.
